Discovery of Exoplanets

NASA's exoplanet exploration program helps us discover planets outside our solar system. People may be able to find magical planets that nurture life among them.

What is the interactive narrative about

This page allows users to interactively explore the data collected by NASA and compare the properties of these exoplanets with those of the Earth.

Storyline:

  • Scene 1 : Overview of exoplanets discovered by years. The user can get to the next scene by clicking on a specific planet in the scatter plot.
  • Scene 2 : Detailed view of the selected exoplanets characteristics comparing it with earth.
  • Scene 3 : Interactive exploration scene where the user can filter or search planets based on their characteristics.
